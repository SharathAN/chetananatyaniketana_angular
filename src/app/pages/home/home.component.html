What is CNN?

Chetana Natya Niketana (CNN) is an institution where students are trained skilfully in Bharathanatyam, Carnatic music, Veena, Keyboard & Fine Arts. The founder & promoter of CNN, Smt. Chetanasundaresh, trains about 250 students under the art roof of CNN. Students are given enormous opportunity to perform in many fests, cultural programmes & temples.

What is Bharathanatyam?

Bharathanatyam is a classical dance form from the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu, practiced predominantly in modern times by women. The dance is usually accompanied by classical Carnatic music. It dates back to 1000 B.C. This dance form improves body language and brings a new expression to the human face. It is a great physical activity that requires a lot of movement. Children learn to be graceful and well coordinated. Dancing to the beat of Carnatic music also helps their minds relax — a good outlet to stay physically fit and maintain a sound mind.

Carnatic music is a South Indian musical style practised mainly in Tamil Nadu & Karnataka. The songs sung in this style are mainly for Bharathanatyam. The theme of the songs is based on Hindu gods & goddesses. The chief objective of CNN is to spread these Indian traditional arts under a single roof.
